Step 1:

Tap the Curator HD icon.

Step 2:

Sign in with your Username and Password. If you are not currently an InfoNgen subscriber, use your Google Reader account. If you don't have a Google Reader account, you can create a free one at: http://www.google.com/reader.

Step 3:

Navigate to the feed you want to read. If you are using Google Reader, the "All Items" section is going to include any new unread items from all of your Google Reader feeds.

Step 4:

Unread items are in bold text. Tap item headlines to read. Or, you can swipe left and right to navigate to the next and previous items in the feed.

Step 5:

Tapping an item title will navigate to the original web page for the item. Swiping to the right navigates back to the item text from the feed.

Step 6:

To share the current item, tap any of the sharing icons on the toolbar.

Step 7:

To share multiple items, you can select multiple items and then tap the email icon.

Step 8:

You can also select multiple items to add to a newsletter section.
