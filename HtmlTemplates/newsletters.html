Step 1:

Scroll down to the newsletters toolbar. Tap the "Add Newsletter" icon to create a new newsletter: Enter a name and the name of the initial section. You can create as many newsletters as you want.

Step 2:

Open your newsletter: Tap on the newsletter icon and select "Open Newsletter".

Step 3:

Describe your newsletter: Expand the header by tapping the "Expand Header" button. Tap in the box below the newsletter name if you want to include a description or synopsis in the masthead of your newsletter.

Step 4:

Add a custom logo: Tap on the default InfoNgen logo to delete the logo or to replace it with an existing image from your Photo Album.

Step 5:

Select an image from your Photo Album. Move or scale the selected image before tapping "Use" to replace the logo.

Step 6:

Select items for your newsletter: Any of the items under your sources can be added to sections within your newsletter.

Step 7:

Once you select items, they can be added to any section by tapping the area labeled "Tap here to add selected items to this section". You can select as many section headings as you want for each newsletter.

Step 8:

Edit your newsletter: Hit the "Edit" button on the top right to open the edit view. You can now drag headlines around or delete one or more stories.

Step 9:

To rearrange stories in each section, drag the headlines up or down. To delete stories, select multiple headlines and then tap Delete or Keep. If you tap Keep, all unselected headlines will be deleted. When you are happy with your changes, tap Done and see your edited newsletter.

Step 10:

Add story images: Tap the Synopsis button to see your newsletter in synopsis view, which displays the content of the story as well as the headline. You can add images to your stories in synopsis view.

Step 11:

Add an existing image from your Photo Album or automatically fetch images from the item's web page. Or, add an image from the source article's web page by tapping the headline, going to the web page and tapping any image, which will be imported into the newsletter.

Step 12:

Selected images will be resized to a reasonable size if they are too large.

Step 13:

When choosing images from the original web page, multiple images will be listed for selection. Only one image is allowed per story in the newsletter.

Step 14:

Once an image is added to a story, the synopsis text will automatically flow around the image.

Step 15:

Enter your own comments: Tap the blue disclosure button on the right side of each story to show the item edit page. You can edit the synopsis text and enter your own comments. You can also enter HTML tags if you know how.

Step 16:

Your comments will show up underneath the story synopsis next to the quote icon.

Step 17:

To publish or preview your newsletter, tap the action button on the upper right.

Step 18:

To preview your newsletters HTML without publishing it, tap the "Show Preview" button. The preview renders the newsletter's HTML using the iPad's built-in web browser. If the newsletter looks good, its ready to publish!

Step 19:

Tapping "Publish Newsletter" will display a popup email window with the newsletter as the body of the message. If you added any images, they will be uploaded to InfoNgen server so that recipients of your newsletter will be able to access the images. Add as many email recipients as you want. Hit Send and it will be emailed to the list. The published newsletter will appear in the sent folder of your email account.

Step 20:

In the Newsletters toolbar, you will see all the newsletters you have created. Tap the newsletter icon to see the action sheet. Using the action sheet, you can create new sections, add selected items to each section, add the newsletter to the favorites toolbar, and delete the newsletter.
